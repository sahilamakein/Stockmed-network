About Seller

Stockmed.in is an online B2B Platform of Stockmed Network Private Limited developed for Indian Healthcare Industry to offer their products to Indian and world buyers. Our portal is an ideal platform for buyers and sellers across the globe to interact and conduct business with increased cost effectiveness and fastest demand fulfillment. Indian Medical products and companies are promoted on the marketplace through effective online presence of their profile and products.

The company is promoted by leaders who have over two decades of experience in Healthcare Industry and International Trade. We understand there is growing need of providing a focused online B2B platform for the Indian Medical and Allied Products Industry for promotion of their products to the global market and make Micro and SME and Large Industry both accessible to Large International Customers on single platform.
